Denne oppgaven skriver ut i konsollen.

For å åpne konsollen kan du:

  • Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+K
  • Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+J
  • Safari: Command+Option+I (the developer menu must be enabled in “Preferences > Advanced”)
  • Opera: Ctrl+Shift+I, click “Console”
  • Internet Explorer: F12, click “Console”
